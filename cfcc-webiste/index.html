About Us

Established in 2018, Clydesdale Farm Cricket Club (CFCC) is based at Elara Oval, Marsden Park, NSW 2765. We are passionate about cricket and community spirit, welcoming players of all ages and abilities.

Club President: Ravi Hemadri

Ravi is dedicated to building a thriving cricket community and guiding CFCC to success both on and off the field.
